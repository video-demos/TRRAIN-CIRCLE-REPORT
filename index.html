Product Overview

Kirana Friends offers a comprehensive mobile app to empower kirana business owners. With its user-friendly interface and robust capabilities, the app serves as a tool for streamlining business operations and maximizing growth potential. Its advanced functionalities ensure users receive tailored support and insights, making it an indispensable asset for their business needs. It empowers kirana owners to stay updated with current trends and offers tailored advice for improving sales based on various factors, including festive periods, location, and other circumstances.

Checkout the application on Google Playstore!

Key Features of the application:

  • Bi-Lingual application with support for Hindi and English content.
  • The shelf analysis tool offers insights to enhance shelf organization based on uploaded pictures.
  • The sales analysis tool provides a detailed report based on .xls files uploaded.
  • The Misraji chatbot is available to answer any questions related to kirana stores.
  • Huge amount of content in the form of articles and reels to enhance knowledge of the users.
  • Charcha (forum) section to ensure user engagement and interaction of the community.
  • The calendar section keeps users informed about upcoming festivals and advises on high-demand items, helping kirana owners maximize sales.
  • The courses section features video tutorials that impart valuable knowledge to users. It also includes certificate generation and sharing to keep users motivated and engaged.
  • Calculator section helps users get an estimate of salt circle, sales potential based on store size, location and city, recommended store renovation budget.
  • Compliance section provides information on regulatory compliances kirana owners must make sure to fulfill.
  • Supplier section includes details of some suppliers dealing specific products kirana owners can contact to.
  • Subscription plan (currently free) to access certain content.

Tech Stack

  • React Native: Used to build frontend of the application.
  • NodeJS: Used to build the backend APIs of the application.
  • Firebase: Used to send notifications and implement crashlytics.
  • TypeScript: Used with React Native and ReactJS in order to enhance code maintainability.
  • MongoDB: Database.
  • ReactJS: Used to build the admin panel of the application.
  • Clevertap: Used to implement event listeners in the application allowing admins to view statistics of user attention.
  • AWS S3: Used to store banner images, forum post images, course videos, reels, etc.
  • AWS EC2: Used to deploy the backend of the application.
  • Kaleyra: Used to send OTP messages to the user.
  • Flask: Used to build the backend of AI services.

My contributions in the mobile app

I have developed a suite of innovative features for the app to enhance user engagement and streamline operations. Key features include an engaging reels section, in-app share, a robust YouTube-like video player for courses, seamless certificate generation and sharing. Users can effortlessly share and repost content within the app, thanks to the in-app share functionality.

To ensure a smooth user experience, I implemented OTP verification with autoread, allowing for quick and secure logins. The app also includes a comprehensive bookmarking system and real-time notifications for comments on posts, keeping users connected and informed.

Infinite scrolling has been integrated throughout the app, providing a fluid and continuous browsing experience.

On the backend, I designed and implemented APIs to support all app functionalities, ensuring reliable and efficient performance.

Furthermore, I have integrated advanced AI services, including sales analysis, shelf analysis, and the Misraji chatbot, to offer users valuable insights and assistance. These features collectively make the app a powerful tool for kirana business owners, helping them manage and grow their businesses with ease.
